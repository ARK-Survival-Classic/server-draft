A Return to What ARK Was Meant to Be

ARK Survival Classic

None of this modern ARK nonsense. I never even played it. We're not making another ARK server. We're reviving ARK: Survival Evolved 2017, the version people actually fell in love with.

Join the Discord Read the Vision
A dinosaur game should never have become this complicated. Dinosaurs are the selling point — not flying whales with lasers.

This server isn't built for the modern ARK player grinding the latest meta. It's built for those who haven't played in years. For those who remember when bases were on the ground. When cave spots mattered. When obelisk warfare was the endgame. When you'd farm with your tribe of four and feel like you'd earned something real.


This is ARK: Survival Classic — a 90-day wipe cycle designed towards and balanced for people with less time to play, built around slow progression, fair protection, and the understanding that everyone deserves the chance to build a crap pillar base and tame a negative melee giga without getting wiped by day two.

Design Philosophy

Core Pillars

Every rule, every system, every decision works toward these principles.

🦖

Dinosaurs First

No cryopods. No Managarmr. No Snow Owls healing through freeze. The creature meta returns to what it was — Rexes, Spinos, Carnos, Pteras. Tames you actually had to work for and protect. The game is about dinosaurs. We're keeping it that way.

🏕️

Land Bases, Real Spots

When every base spot matters again, tribes spread out. Caves become contested. Cliff platforms, hidden valleys, and that weird spot behind the waterfall you'd never consider in modern ARK — they all become viable again. We want to see the whole map populated, not just the five meta spots.

⏳

Slow Burn, Safe Start

Progression is gated week by week. Level caps, building limits, and tame caps increase in increments so no tribe can end-game rush on day one and wipe the server. Everyone gets time to settle, build, farm, and grow — at a pace that works for people with actual lives.

⚔️

Fair Warfare

First 24 hours after wipe are open PvP — contest your base spot, fight for your ground. After that, a 48-hour raid lock kicks in so everyone can settle. Auto turrets come online Week 1. Heavy turrets don't arrive until Week 3. The siege phase is earned, not rushed.

🤝

Built for Workers

Tribe limit of 4. Employed players can verify and receive a special shop. This isn't designed for greaseheads pulling 16-hour days. It's designed for the player who has actual responsibilities now.

🗺️

Era-Authentic Maps

At the end of the 3 month wipe everyone's character is reset and maps released after 2017 will be added to the cluster each month in the same order they originally launched, following the WoW Classic model. Each month after the wipe introduces another chapter of ARK history as it actually happened. You'll re-live the timeline, not skip to the end of it.

Player Progression

Week-by-Week Unlock

Level caps, building limits, and tame caps increase each week. No one gets to skip the journey.

Week 1 — The Land Grab
Foundations
Level Cap: 68 Auto Turrets Online 24hr Open PvP → 48hr Raid Lock

Wipe day hits. The first 24 hours are open warfare — contest base spots, fight for position, claim your ground. After that, raid lock activates for 48 hours so every tribe can settle in. Auto turrets are available from the start so real defences are possible early. No heavy turret nonsense. Opening PvP feels fair, scrappy, and honest. One base claim per tribe — no spam pillaring areas. Anyone caught foundation-spamming during raid lock earns a 7-day ban.

Week 2 — The Economy
War Machine Ramps Up
Level Cap: 82 Vault Unlocked Industrial Forge Chemistry Bench

The war economy begins. Industrial-scale production comes online. Tribes start stockpiling, forming alliances, negotiating trades. Vaults mean your loot finally has real protection. The trading marketplace (Discord bot) opens for business — list items, browse offers, fill orders, and negotiate deals through a structured system that keeps the community connected.

Week 3 — The Siege Phase
Proper War Begins
Level Cap: 100 Heavy Turrets Unlocked

Now it gets real. Heavy turrets arrive, established bases become genuine fortresses, and the server enters the proper siege era. By now, every tribe has had fair time to build up. Raids are earned through preparation, not through getting online faster than everyone else on day one. This is where alliances are tested and wars begin for real.

Week 4 — New Frontier
Aberration Opens
Level Cap: 115 Aberration Map Launches 48hr Raid Lock on New Map

A brand new map joins the cluster. Aberration opens with its own 48-hour raid lock, giving everyone a fair shot at the underground. New tames, new resources, new base spots. A meaningful progression bump for those ready to venture deeper. The cluster starts to feel alive across multiple maps.

Week 6+ — Endgame
Final Chapter
Level Cap: 120 End-Wipe Cap

The final stretch. Maximum level reached, building and tame caps fully unlocked. The last weeks of the wipe are the culmination of everything built before — obelisk control, cross-map raiding, territory wars, and the kind of server-wide politics that only happen when everyone's been grinding the same cycle together. Then it all resets and the next era begins.

Server Law

The Rules

Simple. Non-negotiable. Designed to keep the playing field level.

01

One Base Per Tribe

You may claim one base location. Build around it, defend it, love it. No spam-pillaring secondary locations, no walling off resources, no land-grabbing empires.

02

No Foundation Spam

Spam structures outside your base perimeter are prohibited. Only build around your claimed location. Violations during raid lock result in a 7-day ban — no warnings.

03

Tribe Limit: 4 Players

Small tribes keep the balance. No mega-tribe alliances steamrolling the map. Four players, that's your army. Coordinate with allies if you want, but your roster stays at four.

04

Monthly Character Reset

Every month, characters reset. This keeps progression cycling and prevents permanent power gaps between veterans and newcomers joining mid-wipe.

05

Respect the Raid Lock

During raid lock windows (24h after each wipe and new map launch), all raiding is prohibited. This is your safe window to establish, build, and prepare. Violators face immediate action.

06

Incremental Build & Tame Caps

Building piece limits and tame caps increase weekly. You can't build a mega-fortress in week one. Progression is collective — the whole server grows together.

The WoW Classic Model

Wipe Era Roadmap

At the end of each 90-day cycle, characters reset. Post-2017 maps are then added to the cluster once per month in the order they originally launched — reliving ARK history as it happened. This roadmap will only be activated if demand for ARK: Survival Classic exceeds capacity on the original cluster.

Post-Wipe Month 1
Extinction + New Cluster
+ Extinction November 2018 — Paid Expansion 24hr Open Warfare 48hr Raid Lock

At the end of the standard 3-month wipe, a brand new cluster is created containing the original maps — The Island, The Center, Scorched Earth, Ragnarok, and later Aberration. Extinction is the first post-2017 expansion to join this new cluster, bringing Earth back into the equation — corrupted creatures, orbital supply drops, and titan fights. 24 hours of open warfare followed by a 48-hour raid lock so everyone gets a fair start.

Post-Wipe Month 2
Valguero
+ Valguero June 2019 — Free Official Map 48hr Raid Lock

A free official map with diverse biomes, underground cave systems, and the Deinonychus. Valguero expands the cluster with fresh base spots and new territory to contest.

Post-Wipe Month 3
Genesis: Part 1
+ Genesis: Part 1 February 2020 — Paid Expansion Mission-Based Structure 48hr Raid Lock

The expansion pass content begins. Genesis: Part 1 introduced a mission-based structure — a departure from classic ARK. Added to the cluster in its original form as part of the historical timeline.

Post-Wipe Month 4
Crystal Isles
+ Crystal Isles June 2020 — Free Official Map 48hr Raid Lock

Another free official map joins the rotation. Crystal Isles brings floating islands, crystal wyverns, and a visually distinct landscape to the growing cluster.

Post-Wipe Month 5
Genesis: Part 2
+ Genesis: Part 2 June 2021 — Final Paid Story Expansion 48hr Raid Lock

The final paid story expansion for ARK: Survival Evolved. Genesis: Part 2 closes out the original storyline and enters the cluster as the last premium content drop.

Post-Wipe Month 6
Lost Island
+ Lost Island December 2021 — Free Official Map 48hr Raid Lock

A community-inspired free map featuring new creatures and varied terrain. Lost Island adds another layer of base spots and resource variety to the cluster.

Post-Wipe Month 7
Fjordur
+ Fjordur June 2022 — Final Free Official Map 48hr Raid Lock

The final major free official map for ARK: Survival Evolved. Fjordur marks the end of the roadmap — after this, the full history of ASE has been relived. The cycle resets and it all begins again.

In-Community Economy

The Trading Marketplace

A structured Discord trading bot. No backroom deals — everything goes through the system.

01

List or Browse

Post items for sale with category, price, and your trader tier (I–IV). Or browse the marketplace and search by item type — weapons, blueprints, misc.

02

Offer or Fill

Click "Offer" to open a negotiation channel with the seller. Or post "Fill Orders" — buy requests that alert traders when someone can supply what you need.

03

Negotiate

Private trade channels open between buyer and seller. Accept, deny, or counter buttons. Channels auto-close after 15 minutes of inactivity. No wasted time.

04

Complete Trade

Both parties confirm via trade-success or trade-failed buttons. Reports available if needed. Listings expire after 3 days. Everything is tracked, transparent, and community-driven.

Why Force the System? All trades go through the marketplace. No bypassing it with friend invites and side deals. This keeps the economy visible, fair, and community-facing. Colour-coded UI, searchable categories, and easy item-selection buttons make it fast to use — not a chore.
